Medical Term Operation

In this activity, you will be asked to split a term into its word parts.

To do so, hover over the text and click in between the characters where you believe the word parts should be separated.

Do not include the split (slash marks) between word roots and combining vowels.

If you would like to undo your work, select Reset Word to start again.

When you are ready, select Check Answer to review the correct word parts and move to the next stage.

After you have split the term, identify the correct meaning of each word part.

When you have identified the meaning, click Check Answer.
