Medical Term Operation

In this activity, you will be asked to split a term into its word parts.

Hover over the text and click in between the characters where you believe the word parts should be separated.

Do not include the split (slash marks) between word roots and combining vowels.

Select Reset Word to start again. Select Check Answer when ready.

After splitting the term, identify the correct meaning of each word part.

Finally, record yourself pronouncing the term, then compare with the correct pronunciation.
